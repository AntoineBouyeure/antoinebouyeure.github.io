I am a neuroscientist working on traumatic memory, fear learning, autobiographical memory, and ultra-high-field neuroimaging, with a particular interest in hippocampal function, representational analyses, and laminar fMRI.

My work combines cognitive neuroscience, computational analysis, and advanced neuroimaging methods to study how memory representations are formed, transformed, generalized, and extinguished.
